About Me

CREATIVE STORYTELLER
& DIGITAL CREATOR

I am Kenneth Vongrim Ponfa — a Director, Editor, and Sound Recordist with a deep passion for visual storytelling. I blend creativity with strategy to produce content that resonates with real people and real emotions.

My work spans documentary film, music videos, commercials, and social content — always anchored in entertainment, culture, and social impact. Whether behind the camera, in the editing room, or developing fresh ideas, I believe powerful stories can educate, entertain, and create change.
